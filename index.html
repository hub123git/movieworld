The Last Leaf

O. Henry

In a small apartment in New York, two young artists, Sue and Johnsy, struggle during a harsh winter. Johnsy falls gravely ill with pneumonia and loses the will to live, believing she will die when the last leaf falls from a vine outside her window. Their elderly neighbor, Behrman, a failed but kind-hearted artist, hears of her belief. One night during a storm, he paints a realistic leaf on the wall to give Johnsy hope.
